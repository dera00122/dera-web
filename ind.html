FAQ

Common questions

How long does a project take?

A basic business website typically takes 1–2 weeks. School portals and custom applications take 3–6 weeks depending on scope, discussed clearly during discovery.

Do you offer support after launch?

Yes. Every project includes an initial support window, and ongoing maintenance plans are available so your site keeps running smoothly and gets updated as needed.

Can I request changes during development?

Absolutely. You'll review the design before development starts, and we build in checkpoints so you can give feedback along the way.

Do you work with clients outside Nigeria?

Yes. We work with clients internationally through WhatsApp, email, and video calls, with quotes available in USD, GBP, or EUR alongside naira.

What do I need to get started?

Just a clear idea of your business or institution and your goals. We'll guide you through the rest — content, domain, and hosting — on our first call.

Ready to bring your idea to life?

Tell us about your project and we'll respond within a few hours.
